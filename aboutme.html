I'm Zahirah

Zahirah Salma Nuha is a final-year Master of Design and Production student at the University of Melbourne, Victorian College of The Arts, specialising in Graphic Design. In 2023, She graduated from Institut Teknologi Sepuluh Nopember (ITS) in Indonesia with Visual Communication Design as the 8th most competitive major at ITS. She earned recognition as the fastest and most outstanding Bachelor of Design graduate. Zahirah is passionate about animation, design, storytelling, and illustration. Her work has gained recognition in Indonesia through several notable competition wins, reflecting her dedication and creative skills.
